# Features

## Daily Signals

Daily Signals is the central intelligence feed. It aggregates signals from multiple public source categories, normalizes them, and presents the most relevant items in a ranked format.

Publicly documented behavior:

- Multi-source signal aggregation.
- Ranking based on recency, credibility, diversity, momentum, and impact-oriented metadata.
- Source status indicators for live, cached, fallback, or demo states.
- Related concepts and follow-up paths.
- Compact dashboard presentation for fast scanning.

## Paper Analysis

The paper analysis workflow helps users triage research papers. It focuses on extracting useful structure from dense technical documents.

Publicly documented behavior:

- Paper upload or text-based analysis workflow.
- Input constraints for safety and cost control.
- Structured results such as contribution, limitations, implications, and follow-up actions.
- Saved analysis history for later review.
- Navigation from paper insights into concept exploration.

## Research Papers

The research papers section surfaces recently published or newly relevant papers and connects them to the broader intelligence workflow.

Publicly documented behavior:

- Paper cards with freshness indicators.
- Related concept links.
- System relevance notes.
- Entry points into paper analysis.

## Concept Explorer

The concept explorer visualizes relationships between technical concepts, signals, papers, and saved research context.

Publicly documented behavior:

- Searchable concept graph.
- Focused node detail panel.
- Evidence-backed relationships.
- Navigation from signals and papers into related topics.
- Optional semantic assistance in private implementation.

## Decision Engine

The decision engine helps compare research directions against a defined focus. It is intended to make prioritization more deliberate and inspectable.

Publicly documented behavior:

- Candidate research direction scoring.
- Weighted criteria such as signal strength, graph relevance, research gap, novelty, and evidence quality.
- Workspace-style UI for comparing options.
- Deterministic fallback behavior when AI scoring is unavailable.
